Just glance up.

A quiet pill at the top of your screen. Hover — it expands into live bars, reset countdowns and your plan. Click a provider to pin it.

04

Built to run all day.

  • Refreshes every 60s, on hover, and right after your Mac wakes
  • API rate-limited? Backs off 5 minutes — and keeps your last good data on screen
  • Token expired? It says so in words, not a blank dash
  • Clicks fall through the empty space — it never steals your mouse
  • One tap on startup and it's there every login
